Create a premium SaaS product that feels like a combination of Linear, Notion, Ramp, Stripe Dashboard, and Raycast, while maintaining its own identity.

The design must be:

* Minimalist
* Modern

* Highly accessible
* Enterprise-ready
* Visually impressive without being flashy
* Clean information hierarchy
* Responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile
* Designed for power users who spend hours daily in the platform

* Strong contrast ratios for accessibility (WCAG compliant)

* Beautiful typography hierarchy
* Professional iconography
* Refined micro-interactions
